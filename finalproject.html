Discover Tourist Attraction of Can Tho - Tay Do City

Introduction to Can Tho city

Can Tho is the center of the Mekong Delta, famous for its floating markets, lush fruit gardens and delicious food.

Top 5 must-visit places in Can Tho

  1. Cai Rang Floating Market
  2. Bang Lang Stork Garden
  3. Binh Thuy Ancient House
  4. Ong Pagoda
  5. Ninh Kieu Wharf

This content below will clarify these places. Watch it out!!!

Cai Rang Floating Market

Cai Rang floating market is one of the three biggest floating markets in the Mekong Delta. The market is open all day but it is busiest from sunrise to about 9 am. The main items sold there are farm products and specialties of Cai Rang Town, Chau Thanh District and neighboring areas. Every boat has a long upright pole at its bow on which samples of the goods for sale are hung. The waterway becomes a maze of hundreds of boats packed with mango, bananas, papaya, pineapple, and even smuggled goods like cigarettes. Sellers tie their goods to a tall pole so that buyers can see from a distance what they are selling.

Bang Lang Stork Garden

Bang Lang Stork Garden is located in Thot Not District, about 60 kilometers from Can Tho City. The garden is home to thousands of storks, herons, and other bird species. The best time to visit the garden is from December to June when the birds come to nest. The garden is a great place for bird watching and photography.

Binh Thuy Ancient House

Binh Thuy Ancient House is located in Binh Thuy Ward, about 10 kilometers from Can Tho City. The house was built in 1870 by the Duong family. The house is a fine example of a wealthy rice trader's house in the Mekong Delta in the 19th century. The house is a blend of Vietnamese, French, and Chinese architectural styles. The house is surrounded by a lush garden with many fruit trees. The house was used as a location for the film "The Lover" based on the novel by Marguerite Duras.

Ong Pagoda

Ong Pagoda is located in Ninh Kieu District, Can Tho City. The pagoda was built in 1894 by the Chinese community. The pagoda is dedicated to Quan Cong, a Chinese general who is worshipped as a symbol of loyalty, sincerity, integrity, and justice. The pagoda is a fine example of Chinese architecture with a main hall, a central courtyard, and a bell tower. The pagoda is surrounded by a lush garden with many bonsai trees.

Ninh Kieu Wharf

Ninh Kieu Wharf is located in Ninh Kieu District, Can Tho City. The wharf is a popular place for locals and tourists to relax and enjoy the view of the Hau River. The wharf is lined with restaurants, cafes, and souvenir shops. The wharf is a great place to watch the sunset over the river. The wharf is also the starting point for boat tours of the Mekong Delta.
