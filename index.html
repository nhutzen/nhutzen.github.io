1. Cai Rang Floating Market

Cai Rang Floating Market is one of the most famous and largest floating markets in the Mekong Delta, Vietnam. It is a vibrant and bustling market where traders sell a variety of goods directly from their boats.

2. Bang Lang Stork Garden

Bang Lang Stork Garden is a popular eco-tourism destination in Can Tho, Vietnam. It is home to thousands of storks and other bird species, offering visitors a chance to observe these birds in their natural habitat.

3. Binh Thuy Ancient House

Binh Thuy Ancient House is a well-preserved French colonial house in Can Tho, Vietnam. It showcases the unique blend of Vietnamese and French architectural styles and offers a glimpse into the lifestyle of the wealthy landowners in the early 20th century.

4. Ong Pagoda

Ong Pagoda is a Chinese-style temple located in Can Tho, Vietnam. It is dedicated to the worship of Kuang Kung, a deity symbolizing loyalty, justice, reason, intelligence, honor, and courage. The pagoda is known for its vibrant architecture and cultural significance.

5. Ninh Kieu Wharf

Ninh Kieu Wharf is a popular tourist destination in Can Tho, Vietnam. It is a bustling waterfront area known for its scenic views, vibrant night market, and boat tours along the Hau River. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll along the promenade, dine at riverside restaurants, and experience the local culture.
